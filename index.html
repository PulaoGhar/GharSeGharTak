There are a lot of restaurants who are offering desi food as well as western, etc. as per my experience these restaurants are offering much better and testy food compare to the business class expensive restaurants where customer’s first priority is to experience the environment not just to have meal. But If it comes to small desi restaurants people come to enjoyed the food, and the unique test. Some of the famous restaurants which are falling in this category are Butt Karahi Lakshmi Showk, Phaja k Siri Paye in Wall city, Hafiz juice corner old Anarkali, Mohammadi Nehari, Waqas Biryani Hall road, Chaman Ice cream etc. there are some international franchises that are operating in Lahore such as, fork & knife, salt & paper, KFS, Hardies, MacDonald’s etc. all these are contributing to the food industry in Lahore. Now let’s talk about Pulao Ghar, as per the name this business will also fall in to desi restaurants who will offer local desi food. Pulao ghar’s core dish will be Bannu Pulao, which a cultural food of Bannu. And Banochi people make this dish on special occasion. The specialty of this dish is that it will be only cook in the spices made in Bannu. Bannu people like to cook Pulao in Beef, but now people are also making it with chicken. There are some other accessories which will be serves or offers by road side vendors as side dish along with the Beef Pulao, such as Podine ka raita, Alu ka Achar, Red Lobia, Channay etc. and we will also try to provide the same accessories along with the main dish. The reason of implementing Pulao Ghar idea was the pandemic. I have observed that due to the pandemic people were isolated to their homes and were not able to go out and eat quality food. But only the delivery boys were allowed to get their and deliver food from their ordered restaurant. So, Pulao ghar flash In to my mind what why I shouldn’t I start my own business. because I have the skills to cook, and have place to start the business. and have enough capital to initiate the idea. Then I have started thinking and planning to how to start, how to generate capita, how to get someone to help me. And as a result, this Business plan has been generated, which is the logical form of what I was thinking, what are my goals, then what would be the methodology by which I will have to achieve my goals. During generating this business plan, I have thought that I must need someone to help me, that is why I have asked my friends to help me out and in return they can get a specific share from the net profit for a specific time period. So, there are 5 other members who will be the limited partners in the business. Their roles and responsibilities and shares in the company has been discussed in details in the business plan. I hope that Pulao ghar will be an opportunity for all of us, and will soon get a respectable position in the food industry in Lahore.
